Newborn Incubator Guidelines

Humidity Levels

For optimal care, incubators should maintain a humidity level of 40% - 60%. For preterm infants, a higher humidity level of up to 85% may be needed in the first few days to prevent dehydration.

Normal Heart Rate

The normal heart rate for newborns ranges between 120 and 160 beats per minute (bpm). If the heart rate is too high or too low, medical intervention may be required.
